How to store cabbage

Generally

Store cabbage in the fridge. Do not wash the cabbage until you are going to use it. The whole head can be wrapped in plastic or stored in a container and placed in the crisper drawer. If the cabbage is cut, place it in an airtight container to retain moisture.

Do not store with

Apples, bananas, onions and garlic. Learn more about what not to store together.
