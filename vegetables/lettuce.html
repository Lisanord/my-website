How to store lettuce

Generally

Store lettuce in the refrigerator to maintain its freshness and crispness. Avoid washing the leaves until you're ready to use them. To keep the lettuce fresh, wrap it in a dry, clean towel to absorb any moisture, then place it in a container or plastic bag. This helps prevent the leaves from becoming soggy. Alternatively, if you have a whole head of lettuce, you can cut off the bottom of the stem, place the lettuce in a container, and fully submerge the head in water. This method helps keep the lettuce hydrated and extends its shelf life. Be sure to change the water every couple of days for best results.

Do not store with

Fruits, tomatoes and onions. Learn more about what not to store together.
