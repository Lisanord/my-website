How to store cucumbers

Generally

Store cucumbers in the refrigerator. Rinse them with water and dry thoroughly with a clean towel to remove excess moisture, which can cause them to spoil. Wrap the cucumbers in a dry towel and place them in a mesh produce bag to allow proper airflow. If you've already sliced the cucumber, store the pieces in an airtight plastic bag or container to keep them fresh. For the best results, store them in the crisper drawer, which provides the ideal humidity level for vegetables.

Do not store with

Fruits, potatoes, tomatoes and melons. Learn more about what not to store together.
