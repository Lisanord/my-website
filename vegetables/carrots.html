How to store carrots

Generally

Store carrots in the refrigerator. Avoid washing or peeling them until you're ready to use, as moisture can lead to quicker spoilage. Remove the tops and greens, as they can draw moisture from the carrots. Gently dry the carrots with a clean towel, then store them in a sealed container or plastic bag. Place a clean towel at the bottom to absorb any excess moisture and maintain freshness.

Do not store with

Fruits, potatoes and onions. Learn more about what not to store together.
