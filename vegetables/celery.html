How to store celery

Generally

Store celery in the fridge. Wrap the whole celery in foil and make sure none of the celery is exposed. This will help the celery retain its moisture and let the gases it produses naturally to escape. It will last in the fridge for three to four weeks.

Do not store with

Apples, bananas and tomatoes. Learn more about what not to store together.
