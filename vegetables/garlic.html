How to store garlic

Generally

Store garlic in a cool, dry, and well-ventilated area, such as a cupboard or on the counter, away from direct sunlight. Place it in a breathable container like a paper bag, mesh bag, wire basket, or a ceramic bowl designed for garlic storage. These options allow air circulation and help keep garlic fresh longer. Garlic is often stored with onions, as they share similar storage conditions, but it's best to keep them in separate containers to avoid odor transfer.

Do not store with

Fruits, potatoes and carrots. Learn more about what not to store together.
