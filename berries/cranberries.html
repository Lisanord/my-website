How to store cranberries

Generally

Store cranberries in the fridge. If they are already packaged in a plastic bag, they can just be placed in the crisper drawer. If not, wash them with cold water and transfer to an airtight container. They can last like this for more than three weeks.

Do not store with

Bananas, apples and leafy greens. Learn more about what not to store together.
