How to store blueberries

Generally

Store blueberries in the refrigerator. Before storing, wash the blueberries gently with cold water and dry them thoroughly to prevent excess moisture, which can cause mold.Place the blueberries in a clean container or jar, lining the bottom with a paper towel to absorb any excess moisture and help keep the berries dry. This will help extend their shelf life and keep them fresh for as long as possible.

Do not store with

Apples, bananas and citrus fruits. Learn more about what not to store together.
