How to store mangoes

Generally

If the mangoes are unripe, store them on the counter at room temperature. Once ripe, they can be stored in the fridge loose, in a mesh bag or a bowl to promote air flow. This will slow further ripening.

Do not store with

Leafy greens, berries and cucumbers. Learn more about what not to store together.
