How to store pineapples

Generally

An uncut pineapple can be stored on the counter for up to three days. To store for longer, put a whole pineapple in the warmest part of the fridge for up to six days. Alternativly, cut the pineapple and store in an airtight container.

Do not store with

Bananas, apples, onions and garlic. Learn more about what not to store together.
