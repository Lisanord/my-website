How to store grapes

Generally

Store grapes in the fridge. If you have big clusters, cut them into smaller bushes. This is just for practicalitys sake. Wash the clusters with water and vinegar. Dry them thoroughly and place in a container or jar with a paper towel at the bottom.

Do not store with

Apples, bananas and tomatoes. Learn more about what not to store together.
