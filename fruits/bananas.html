How to store bananas

Generally

Store bananas on the counter at room temperature, away from direct sunlight. To help slow down the ripening process, wrap aluminum foil around the stems. This helps to reduce the amount of ethylene gas that the bananas release, which in turn slows their ripening.

Do not store with

Leafy greens, apples and carrots. Learn more about what not to store together.
